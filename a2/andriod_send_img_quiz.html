Question 2 - How to open up the photo library to send an image to someone?



The answer is b.
1. Open the blue app which has a text bubble icon inside it.
2. Click the contacts button, the page should now open up.
3. Scroll through until you find the person you are planning to send an image to. Click on their name.
4. Once opened, click on the plus symbol on the bottom left-hand corner of the screen.
5. A variety of options would appear, find the icon that says 'Image'.
6. Click on the icon and it will lead you to your gallery.

That's correct!!

Question 3 - Once in the photo library how do you send the images to someone?




The answer is a.
1. Once you have selected your image, it should have a tick on it. Proceed to press the done button.
2. It will lead you back to your contact, now click on the green arrow to send.


That's correct!!

Question 1 - Which app is the Phone app?





The answer is c.
The Phone app is green with a phone in the centre and there is no speech bubble around it.


That's correct!!

Question 2 - How do you dial a phone number?



The answer is a.
1. Click on the Phone app which is green with a white phone in the center.
2. Click on the keypad with the 9 nine dots on the bottom of the screen.
3. Enter in the phone number.
4. Click the green button to call.

That's correct!!

Question 3 - How to call someone from your contacts?




The answer is b.
1. Click on the Phone app which is green with a white phone in the center.
2. Click on the contacts with the two people on the bottom of the screen.
3. Scroll through your contacts and select who you want to call.
4. On your contact profile, select call then select the number you want to call them by.
5. This should then commence the call.
6. To end the call press the red button.


That's correct!!

Question 4 - How do you end the call?




The answer is b.
To end a call press the red circle button with a phone symbol


That's correct!!

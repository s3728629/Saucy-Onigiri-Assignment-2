Step by Step Instructions

Step 1

Click on the Messages app which is green with a white speech bubble in the center.

Step 2

Select the person who you would like to send the image to.

Step 3

At the bottom of the screen, there will be a whole bar of icons.

Step 4

Press the white icon with a rainbow flower in the center, just above your keyboard.

Step 5

This will then open your photo library and you can choose the photo or video you wish to send.

Step 6

The photo or video will then appear in the message bar, to the right of it, press the send button which is an arrow.

Step 7

After the image or video has been sent, under it, there should be the word "delivered" shown in dark grey font.

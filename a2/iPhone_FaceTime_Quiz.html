Question 2 - How do you dial someone in FaceTime?



The answer is b.
1. Click on the FaceTime app which is green with a video recorder in the center.
2. Click the ‘add’ button on the top right of the screen.
3. Click the small round button with an add symbol in the middle.
4. Scroll through your contacts and choose who you want to call.
5. Click the third circle option which is labelled ‘video’.
6. The call should commence.

That's correct!!

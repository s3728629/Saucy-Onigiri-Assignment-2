Welcome To Stay Connected

To get you started and prepared, you have the option to take an Intro quiz to create the best experience for you! This page is a tutorial that teaches you how to use a quiz. After this page is the pre-quiz.
In order to read through this tutorial, you must read through the the Steps on the left with the corresponding animated images that support each step on the right.
If you do not know how to take an online quiz, follow the steps below. Otherwise, if you are already aware of how to complete a quiz, click on the 'Skip' button below.

Step 1

To be able to answer questions in the quiz, read the questions in big text in blue and different options provided below.


Step 2

For the first two questions, click on the blue tabs and click on the desired option provided within the list.


Step 3

For the last option, click on one the checkbox next to the answer that you wish to choose for the question.

Step 4

To finish and continue, click the 'Finished' Button below on the bottom right of the page.


Once you have finished, click the button

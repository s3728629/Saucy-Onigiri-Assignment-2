Question 2 - How to call someone from your contacts?



The answer is b.
1. A list of your contacts will then be listed by alphabetical order.
2. To make a call, scroll down until you find the person you are wanting to talk to.
3. Once you have clicked on their name, three buttons will appear under their name.
4. Proceed to click the green phone icon to start the call.

That's correct!!

Step by Step Instructions

Step 1

Click on the white coloured app that has a red and white envelope icon in the center.

Step 2

After opening, you would first be greeted by a list of your primary emails.

Step 3

To send an email, click on the compose button found at the bottom right hand corner.

Step 4

Once clicked, it would lead you to a template. At the top right hand corner, there is a paperclip icon.

Step 5

Two options appear once you press it, either 'Attach file' or 'Insert from Drive'.

Step 6

If you would like to attach something directly from your phone, click on 'Attach file'. It should open up your camera roll. Proceed to scroll through your gallery and pick an image.

Step 7

If you would like to attach something from your computer or a shared drive, click on 'Insert from Drive'. There will be a list of options to pick from.

Step 8

After selecting your desired image or document, click on the triangle button on the right hand corner. This will send the email to your desired recipient.

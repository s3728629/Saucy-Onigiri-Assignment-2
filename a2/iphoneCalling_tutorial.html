Step by Step Instructions

Step 1

Click on the Phone app which is green with a white phone in the center.

How to call by keypad:

Step 1

Click on the keypad with the 9 nine dots on the bottom of the screen.

Step 2

Enter in the phone number.

Step 3

Click the green button to call and the call will commence.

How to call by contacts:

Step 1

Click on the contacts with the two people on the bottom of the screen.

Step 2

Scroll through your contacts and select who you want to call.

Step 3

On your contact profile, select the call button with a phone icon.

Step 4

Select Call from the menu and the call will commence.

Final step

To end the call press the red button with a white phone in the middle.

Mini Tutorials

If you are unaware of how to do certain actions within this website, please use these mini tutorials below. You can always come back to this page later on.

Please Click any option you want to learn about by clicking blue rectangle boxes for each corresponding Mini Tutorial to View it.

To print the pdf guides, click on the pdf icon located at the bottom of the tutorial page.

Once a pop up window opens up, click on the 'Print' button to print, and the 'Cancel' button to exit the window.



On the top left hand corner of your browser window, a set of small arrows should be there.

Click on the arrow pointing left and it will lead you to the previous page. Clicking on it more than once will allow you to visit multiple previous pages.



Located under a tutorial is a 'Start Quiz' button that allows you to consolidate your knowledge on the content learned.

Clicking on the button will direct you to the multiple choice quiz and on completion, can be submitted by clicking on the 'Finished' button.



Click on the play button on the video to begin watching. Click on the pause icon to pause he video.

On the bottom right, a small icon with arrows extending outwards allows you to make the video full screen.

You can control the volume of the video by hovering over the volume icon and then using the slider to adjust the volume to your desired preference.



If you have any questions, you can click on the 'Click here for help' button located at the bottom of the tutorials or the 'Help' button on the top right of the webpage.

When directed to the chatbot, type your query into the textbox and send. An automated response will help you.

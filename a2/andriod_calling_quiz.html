Question 2 - How do you dial a phone number?



The answer is a.
1. Under the list of contacts are several buttons.
2. Pick the button on the bottom left corner titled "Keypad".
3. After you have pressed it, the keypad with numbers will appear.
4. Dial in the phone number that you are planning to call.
5. Proceed to click the green phone icon to start the call.

That's correct!!

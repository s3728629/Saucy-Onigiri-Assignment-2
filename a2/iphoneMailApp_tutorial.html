Step by Step Instructions

Step 1

Click on the Mail app icon that is blue with a white envelope in the center.

Step 2

On the screen you should see a menu with a variety of email services. Choose the one you have an email with. If your email service is not shown on the screen click the "other" option which is at the bottom of the list.

Step 3

Enter your email address, then click next to proceed. Note: Your screen layout may differ based on what emailing service you chose.

Step 4

Enter your password and click next to proceed.

Step 5

Now select what information you would like to sync over from your email account. Select by tapping the circle. Green indicates chosen.

Step 6

Your screen should now show your emails on the screen. To view an email: click on the one you wish to view.

Step 7

The screen will then open to the full contents of the email.

Step 8

To view more of the email, place a finger on the screen, drag up and down to view the full email.

Step 9

To head back to other emails, press the back-arrow button at the top left of the screen.

Step 10

To view the next email, click the down arrow (on the right) on the top right of the screen.

Step 11

To view the email before, click the up arrow (on the left) on the top right of the screen.

Step 12

Flagging: By pressing the flag icon, it opens a menu with a Flag, Mark as Unread, Move to Junk and a Notify Me... option.

  • Flag: Makes the email flagged with an orange circle. You can then view flagged emails on this screen.
  • Mark as unread: Makes the email unread with a blue circle.
  • Move to Junk: Moves the email to your junk box.
  • Notify Me...: Lets the user receive notification when other users reply to the email.

Step 13

Moving email: This will open your email categories where you move your selected email.

Step 14

Archive: This sends your email to the archive, which can be accessed from the All Mail found in mailboxes.

Step 15

Back arrow (reply, forward, print): This opens up with the option to let the user reply to the email, forward the email or print the email.

  • Reply: This opens the email in a reply format, where you can add in your message along with the original email. Just type in your reply and hit send to reply to the email.
  • Forward: This opens the email in forwarded format, where you can enter your intended message.
  • Print: This opens printer options, where you can choose your printer, adjust amount of copies and the range of pages. To select which printer to use, press printer then choose your printer. To adjust amount of copies, click minus to decrease and addition to increase. To select the range of pages, scroll through the numbers to choose your intended pages.

Step 16

Compose: This opens a new email which you can compose.

Step by Step Instructions

Step 1

Click on the Messages app which is green with a white speech bubble in the centre.

Step 2

Select a conversation.

Step 3

Press the text bar which should open the keyboard on the bottom half.

Step 4

At the bottom left, hold on the globe button until your list of keyboards shows up.

Or

If there is no globe, press the smiley face button to be led directly to the emoji keyboard and skip step 5.

Step 5

Select the emoji keyboard.

Step 6

Select which emoji you would like to use.

Step 7

Click the arrow on the right side of your screen to send the message.

How to return back to your keyboard

Press the 'ABC' on the bottom left corner to return to your previous keyboard.

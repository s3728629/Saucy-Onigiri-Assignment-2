Step by Step Instructions

Step 1

Open the blue app which has a text bubble icon inside it.

Step 2

Click on the contact that has sent you an image.

Step 3

Once the conversation has opened, you should be able to see the image.

Step 4

Click on the image that you would like to save.

Step 5

The image should enlarge, and the bottom left-hand corner will have the save button.

Step 6

After the save button has been clicked, "attachment saved to phone" should appear.

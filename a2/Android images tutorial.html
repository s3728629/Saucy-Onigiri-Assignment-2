Step by Step Instructions

Step 1

Open the blue app which has a text bubble icon inside it.

Step 2

Click the contacts button, the page should now open up.

Step 3

Scroll through until you find the person you are planning to send an image to. Click on their name.

Step 4

Once opened, click on the plus symbol on the bottom left-hand corner of the screen.

Step 5

A variety of options would appear, find the icon that says ‘Image’.

Step 6

Click on the icon and it will lead you to your gallery.

Step 7

Once you have selected your image, it should have a tick on it. Proceed to press the done button.

Step 8

It will lead you back to your contact, now click on the green arrow to send.

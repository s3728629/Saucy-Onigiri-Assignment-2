Question 2 - How do you attach a file to you email?



The answer is b.
1. On the main body area in your email, press down your finger for a few seconds then lift it up.
2. A black bar with select, select all and quote level should appear.
3. Press the white triangle on the right side of the bar.
4. You will now see two options: Insert Photo or Video and Add Attachment. Select the one that applies to what you would like to attach.
5. If you chose to attach an image or video:
6. Select "All Photos" and select your wanted photo or video.
7. If you chose to add attachment:
8. Select your file.
9. After successfully attaching, fill in the other details and your message, then press send to send your message.

That's correct!!
